Yuan Li

I am a postdoctoral associate at the High-Performance Computing Architectures & Technologies (HPCAT) Lab in George Washington University, under the supervision of Prof. Ahmed Louri. I got my Ph.D. in computer engineering from George Washington University, M.S. in microelectronics from University of Newcastle upon Tyne, and B.S. in physics from University of Science and Technology of China.

My research focuses on developing efficient and flexible communication fabric for heterogeneous manycores, domain-specific accelerators, and chiplet-based systems by incorporating network architecture innovations and emerging interconnect technologies such as silicon photonics.
